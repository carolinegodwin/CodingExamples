About Me

My name is Caroline Godwin and I am a 22 year old Web Developer from Austin, Texas. I have over 10 years experience in Web Development and over 3 years experience in marketing, both digital and print. I specialize in responsive web design and use my combined programming and graphic design knowledge to create user friendly and functional designs. The gap between technology and art can be closed with an exceptional user experience, and my goal is to provide you with designs that make the two seem intertwined.
